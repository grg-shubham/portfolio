The Business Problem

As the business scaled, managing client orders, payment records, and website performance became increasingly difficult. Teams were spending valuable time searching through reports instead of making decisions.

  • Payment Tracking Was Slow:

    The team struggled to quickly identify which clients had paid, which invoices were pending, and where follow-up was required.

  • No Quick Access to Client History:

    Before accepting a new order, staff had no easy way to check a client's previous payment behavior and reliability.

  • Revenue Visibility Was Limited:

    Management could not instantly see which websites, clients, or periods were generating the highest revenue.

  • Historical Records Were Difficult to Find:

    Locating past orders and payment transactions often required manually searching through multiple reports.

  • Decision Making Was Delayed:

    Without centralized information, important business decisions relied on incomplete or outdated data.

The Solution & Business Impact

I developed a centralized reporting dashboard that provides a complete view of orders, payments, client history, and revenue performance from a single location, helping management make faster and more informed business decisions.

  • 40% Faster Payment Follow-Ups

    Teams can instantly identify pending and overdue payments, reducing collection delays and improving cash flow.

  • 75% Less Time Searching Historical Records

    Client orders and payment history can be retrieved within seconds instead of manually reviewing reports.

  • 100% Client Payment Visibility

    Management can review complete payment history before accepting new orders from any client.

  • 30% Faster Business Decisions

    Revenue, orders, and website performance can be analyzed instantly without waiting for manual reports.

  • 60% Reduction in Manual Reporting

    All order and payment information is available in one place, eliminating spreadsheet dependency.

  • Better Revenue Control

    Track revenue by client, website, and date range to identify the most profitable business channels.
