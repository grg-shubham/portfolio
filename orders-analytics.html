The Full Story
Problem → Solution → Real Business Impact
Why the business needed this, what I built, and what measurably improved after launch.

What Was Slowing the Business Down

A growing business with multiple websites and clients had no central place to see orders, payments, or revenue. Every answer required digging through spreadsheets — costing hours of valuable time every week.

  • Payment Follow-Ups Were Slow and Missed

    No quick way to see which invoices were pending or overdue. Follow-up reminders were inconsistent and delayed — directly hurting cash flow every month.

  • Client History Hidden in Spreadsheets

    Before accepting new orders, staff had no easy way to check a client's payment history and reliability — risky clients slipped through because the data was too hard to find quickly.

  • Revenue Visibility Was Essentially Zero

    Management couldn't instantly see which websites or clients generated the most revenue. Strategic decisions were made on gut feeling rather than data.

  • Finding Historical Orders Took 20–30 Minutes

    Locating a specific past order or payment required manually scanning multiple reports — a simple lookup could eat half an hour of productive time.

  • Decisions Made on Incomplete Data

    Without centralized, up-to-date information, management was always working from outdated or partial pictures — slowing decisions and creating costly blind spots.

What I Built to Solve It

I developed a centralized Looker Studio intelligence dashboard consolidating all order, payment, client, and revenue data into a single live report — accessible to management in one click, no technical knowledge required.

  • Payment Status at a Glance

    A dedicated payment tracking view shows every client's status — Paid, Pending, or Overdue — with amounts and due dates visible instantly. The team now follows up the same day, not weeks later.

  • Full Client Payment History Panel

    Search any client and instantly see their complete order and payment history — every transaction, every amount, every status. Unreliable clients are now identified before new orders are accepted.

  • Revenue Analytics by Website, Client & Period

    Revenue breakdown views by website, by client, and by date range — so management can instantly see which channels are most profitable and make data-backed strategic decisions.

  • Order Search in Seconds

    Any historical order or transaction found using simple filters in under 30 seconds — eliminating the 20–30 minute manual search process entirely.

  • Single Source of Truth for All Teams

    Management, accounts, and operations all work from the same live dashboard. No more version conflicts from spreadsheets passed around by email.

Real Business Impact
What Actually Changed After Going Live

Concrete improvements the business experienced once the dashboard was in daily use.

40% Faster Payment Collection

The team identifies overdue payments instantly and follows up the same day. Cash flow improved noticeably within the first month of deployment.

75% Less Time on Data Lookups

Searches that took 20–30 minutes now take under 30 seconds. Staff spend time acting on information — not hunting for it.

Risky Clients Identified Upfront

Management checks client payment history before accepting new orders. Problem clients are flagged before commitments are made, reducing bad debt risk.

Revenue Strategy Became Data-Driven

For the first time, management could see which websites and clients generated the most revenue — enabling them to prioritize the right channels strategically.

60% Less Manual Reporting Work

Reports that previously required hours of spreadsheet assembly are now self-serve in the dashboard — freeing the team for higher-value work.

All Teams Work From One Source

Management, accounts, and operations use the same live data. Misalignment from conflicting spreadsheet versions became a thing of the past.
