The Full Story
Problem → Solution → Real Business Impact
What was broken, how I fixed it, and what measurably changed for the business afterwards.

What Was Breaking the Business

A trading firm handling 3 product categories had no digital control over their inventory. Everything was manual, scattered across Excel files, and completely unreliable — costing time, money, and stock.

  • No Real-Time Stock Visibility

    The operations team had no way to quickly see available units, total stock value, or which items were running low — they had to manually count or search spreadsheets every time.

  • Inventory Leakage With No Paper Trail

    Stock moved in and out with no proper log. There was no way to verify who processed a transaction, when it happened, or what quantity — making it impossible to catch mistakes or theft.

  • Products Running Out Silently

    Popular items would hit zero units with zero warning. Staff discovered shortages only when a customer asked — by then it was too late, causing lost sales and delayed orders.

  • Vendor & Transfer Management Was Chaotic

    No system tracked which vendor supplied which items, or managed stock transfers between locations. Everything depended on memory and scattered files.

What I Built to Solve It

I built a complete custom web application using Google Apps Script, deployed as a live portal connected directly to a Google Sheets database — no additional software costs, no IT infrastructure needed.

  • Live Executive Dashboard with Real-Time Counters

    Dynamic KPI counters showing Total Stock Value (₹18,65,940), Available Units (233), and category breakdowns — updating live so management always has an accurate picture instantly.

  • Timestamped Transaction Log System

    Every Stock In and Stock Out action is automatically recorded with item name, quantity, timestamp, and user account — a full audit trail that catches errors and eliminates leakage.

  • Visual Low-Stock Alert Engine

    A dedicated "Low Stock" panel with colour-coded warnings (e.g. 2/5 units remaining shown in red) so staff can reorder before products run out — not after customers complain.

  • Full Management Control Panel

    Complete admin menu covering Inventory Master, Stock In/Out, Transfers, Vendor tracking, Documents, and System Alerts — all in one portal accessible from any browser.

Real Business Impact
What Actually Changed After Going Live

Concrete, day-to-day improvements the business experienced once the IMS was deployed in production.

Operations Team Works 80% Faster

Tasks that used to take 30–45 minutes of manual spreadsheet work now take under 2 minutes in the portal — stock checks, transaction logging, everything.

Full Audit Trail for Every Transaction

Management can pull up exactly who moved stock, how much, and when — for any item, any date. Disputes resolved in seconds instead of hours of searching.

Zero Surprise Stockouts

The low-stock alert system gives advance warning before items run out — so reorders happen proactively. Lost sales due to empty shelves dropped to near zero.

₹18.6L of Inventory Fully Tracked

Every rupee of stock is accounted for in real time. Management knows the exact inventory value at any moment — enabling smarter purchasing decisions.

Stock Leakage Eliminated

The timestamped transaction log created accountability for every movement. Unexplained stock discrepancies — which were common before — essentially disappeared.

Accessible from Anywhere, Zero Extra Cost

Built entirely on Google Workspace — which the business already used. No new licenses, no server costs. The portal runs in any browser, on any device.
