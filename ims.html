The Business Problem

A trading firm handling various business products (Electronics, Stationery, Furniture) struggled with manually logging transactions across scattered data sheets. This led to serious operational inefficiencies:

  • Blind Stock Allocations:

    The operations team had no quick visual summary of total item configurations or overall valuation metrics, making it difficult to track high-value business assets.

  • Untracked Inventory Leakage:

    Without an active transaction history log, it was nearly impossible to verify who processed specific "Stock In" or "Stock Out" actions, causing balance issues.

  • Unnoticed Stock Depletion:

    The warehouse frequently ran out of popular items (like USB Hubs or components) because they lacked automated alerts to signal when stock hit critical levels.

The Solution & Business Impact

I built a custom web app using Google Apps Script that works as a complete administrative panel. It connects directly with our database sheets to manage and track everything smoothly:

  • Centralized Executive View Control:

    Engineered dynamic dashboard counters showing live counts (Total Value: ₹18,65,940, Available Stock: 233 units) for immediate operational awareness.

  • Automated Stock Logging System:

    Implemented a structured "Recent Transactions" tracking ledger that captures precisely what items left or entered the system, complete with execution timestamps and user accounts.

  • Visual Low-Stock Safeguards:

    Created a dedicated "Low Stock Items" visual status engine that highlights critical levels (e.g., 2/5 units remaining) using active color warnings to stop supply shortages before they happen.

  • Complete Management Control Panel:

    Designed an intuitive system menu panel including Inventory Master, Stock In/Out, Transfers, Vendor tracking, Documents, and System Alerts.
