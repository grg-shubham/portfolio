What I Do & Why It Matters

I'm a Data Analyst with a strong background in MIS reporting, business intelligence, and workflow automation. At Serve Vision Pvt. Ltd. I manage end-to-end data pipelines covering marketing analytics, student lifecycle tracking, fee management, and content publishing — all automated.

Within Serve Vision, I also directly manage data and operations for two organizations: HSIM (Hindustan School of Internet Marketing) and CAP Academy (Cricket Academy of Pathans) — handling everything from admissions and fees to performance tracking and reporting.

My toolkit spans SQL, Python, Advanced Excel, Power BI, Looker Studio, Google Apps Script, and N8N. I'm equally comfortable designing a Looker Studio dashboard, writing a multi-table SQL query, or building an N8N + OpenAI automation workflow from scratch.

I've supported cross-functional teams — Marketing, Accounts, Management, and Operations — reducing their data turnaround time by 2–3 hours per team every week.

What a team gets when they hire me:

  • Automated MIS reports that replace hours of manual Excel work each week
  • Interactive dashboards in Power BI or Looker Studio that management can use without analyst support
  • N8N / Apps Script workflows that connect APIs, databases, and tools with zero custom dev overhead
  • Clean, validated data structures that reduce reporting errors and keep audits stress-free
  • AI-powered pipelines using OpenAI and Whisper APIs for call analytics and content operations
