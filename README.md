## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 1. Inventory Management System (IMS)
- **Stack:** Google Apps Script, Google Sheets DB, Web App Deployment
- **Business Problem:** A trading firm managing Electronics, Stationery, and Furniture had zero digital control over stock — no real-time visibility, no transaction logs, no low-stock alerts. Products ran out silently and inventory leakage went undetected.
- **Solution:** Built a complete custom web portal connected to a Google Sheets database — live KPI counters (₹18.6L inventory value, 233 units), timestamped transaction logs, visual low-stock alerts, vendor tracking, and a full admin control panel.
- **Impact:** ↓ 80% manual operations · ✓ 100% stock visibility · Zero surprise stockouts

### 2. Student & Fee Management System
- **Stack:** Looker Studio, Google Sheets, Real-time Pipeline
- **Business Problem:** A coaching institute was managing admissions and fees through handwritten registers and scattered Excel files. Fee installments were missed constantly, daily reconciliation took 2–3 hours, and the owner had no business visibility.
- **Solution:** Built a centralized live dashboard tracking admissions, installment status (Paid/Upcoming/Overdue), fee collection summaries, and owner KPI view — all updating in real time.
- **Impact:** ↓ 6–8 hrs saved weekly · ↓ 30% fewer follow-ups · Zero missed payments

### 3. Client Order & Payment Intelligence Dashboard
- **Stack:** Looker Studio, Google Sheets, Revenue Analytics
- **Business Problem:** A growing business with multiple websites and clients had no central place to track orders, payment status, or revenue. Finding a historical order took 20–30 minutes and payment follow-ups were constantly missed.
- **Solution:** Built a BI dashboard with payment status tracking (Paid/Pending/Overdue), full client history search, revenue analytics by website/client/period, and order lookup in under 30 seconds.
- **Impact:** ↑ 40% faster follow-ups · ↓ 75% search time · ↓ 60% manual reporting

### 4. Automated MIS & Call Analytics Pipeline
- **Stack:** Whisper API, OpenAI GPT, Python
- **Business Problem:** Hours of client call recordings were sitting unused — no way to extract insights, summaries, or MIS data from audio without manual listening.
- **Solution:** Built an end-to-end automation pipeline that transcribes raw call audio using Whisper API and passes it through GPT to generate structured MIS summaries, ratings, and action items — completely hands-free.
- **Impact:** 5+ hours of audio processed in 20 minutes · ↓ 80% manual reporting

### 5. AI Content Publishing Engine
- **Stack:** N8N, OpenAI API, WordPress REST API, Google Sheets
- **Business Problem:** Publishing localized articles to WordPress required manual writing, formatting, and uploading — time-consuming and inconsistent.
- **Solution:** Built an N8N workflow connecting Google Sheets data with OpenAI API to auto-generate structured articles and publish them directly to live WordPress endpoints.
- **Impact:** Fully automated publishing · ↓ 8–10 hrs/week saved
