Student and Fees Management System

The Business Problem

As the coaching institute grew, managing student admissions, fee collections, and installments became increasingly difficult. Most records were maintained in registers and multiple Excel files, creating confusion and consuming valuable staff time.

  • Fees Were Getting Missed: Staff had to manually check hundreds of records to identify which students had upcoming or overdue installments. Follow-ups were often delayed, resulting in pending collections.
  • Too Much Time Spent on Daily Checking: Every day, the team had to spend hours matching cash and online payments, increasing the chances of mistakes and confusion.
  • No Clear View of Business Performance: The owner could not quickly see how many active students were enrolled, how much revenue was collected, or how much money was still pending.
  • Staff Dependent Operations: Important information was spread across different files and people, making it difficult to manage the business smoothly when staff members were absent.

The Solution & Business Impact

I created a single place where the institute owner and staff could easily track admissions, fee collections, installments, and overall business performance without searching through multiple files.

  • Never Miss a Student Payment: Staff can instantly see which students have pending, upcoming, or completed installments and follow up on time.
  • Faster Fee Collection: Student records can be found in seconds using simple search options, helping the team recover pending fees more efficiently.
  • Less Manual Work: Daily payment tracking becomes much easier, reducing paperwork and saving administrative effort.
  • Complete Business Visibility: The owner can quickly view total students, collected fees, pending dues, and overall growth without waiting for reports from staff.
  • Smoother Day-to-Day Operations: All important information stays organized in one place, allowing the institute to run more efficiently and with fewer errors.
