The real operational pain this solved and what changed for the institute after going live.

What Was Hurting the Institute Daily

As the coaching institute grew its student base, managing admissions and fees through registers and Excel sheets completely broke down. Fee collection became chaotic, staff were overwhelmed, and the owner had no clear picture of the business.

  • Fee Installments Were Being Missed Constantly

    Staff had to manually scan hundreds of student records to identify overdue installments. Follow-ups were delayed or forgotten — and revenue was slipping through the cracks as a result.

  • Daily Payment Reconciliation Was Eating 2–3 Hours

    Every day, staff manually matched cash and online payments against student records. The process was slow, error-prone, and created daily confusion about who had paid and who hadn't.

  • The Owner Had No View of Business Performance

    No quick way to see total active students, collected revenue, or pending dues. Every answer required asking staff and waiting — or digging through files manually.

  • Operations Too Dependent on Individual Staff

    Critical information lived in different files with different people. When a staff member was absent, simple tasks like checking a student's fee status became a bottleneck for the whole office.

What I Built to Fix It

I built a centralized Looker Studio dashboard connected to a Google Sheets data pipeline — giving the institute owner and staff a single live system to manage every aspect of student admissions and fee collection.

  • Live Installment Tracker — Never Miss a Payment

    A dedicated view shows every student's installment status — Paid, Upcoming, or Overdue — in one screen. Staff see who needs follow-up in seconds each morning, without opening a single spreadsheet.

  • Instant Student Search & Fee History

    Any student's complete record — admission date, course, payments made, dues pending — found in seconds using the search filter. Daily reconciliation dropped from hours to minutes.

  • Owner Business Summary Dashboard

    A top-level view showing Total Students, Collected Revenue, Pending Dues, and Monthly Growth — all updating live. The owner has a real-time pulse on the business at all times.

  • Fully Centralized — No Staff Dependency

    All student and fee information in one place. Any staff member can handle any task independently — operations run smoothly even when individual staff members are absent.

Real Business Impact
What Actually Changed After Going Live

Measurable, day-to-day improvements the institute experienced once the system was in production.

6–8 Hours Saved Every Week

Manual payment matching, fee status checks, and report generation — tasks that consumed entire mornings — are now handled by the dashboard in seconds.

Fee Collection Became Reliable

Staff follow up on overdue payments the same day they become due. Revenue collection improved consistently after deployment — missed payments dropped to near zero.

Any Student Record Found in Seconds

What used to take 10–15 minutes of file-digging now takes under 30 seconds. Staff confidence and service quality improved noticeably.

Owner Has Real-Time Business Visibility

For the first time, the owner checks active students, collected fees, and pending dues in one glance — making business decisions with actual data instead of estimates.

30% Fewer Admin Follow-Up Cycles

Centralized data meant fewer back-and-forth calls between staff to find information. Internal communication overhead dropped significantly.

Institute Runs Without File Dependency

No more "ask someone for the file." Every team member can independently access any record, any time — operations became truly self-sufficient.
